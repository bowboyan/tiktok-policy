**TikTok mp4 Poster – Privacy Policy**


Last updated: July 21, 2025

1. **Introduction**
We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when using TikTok mp4 Poster ("the App").

2. **Data Collected**
Via TikTok OAuth, we collect:
- TikTok user ID
- TikTok username
- Access token (encrypted)

No other personal data (e.g., email, phone number) is collected.

3. **Purpose of Data Use**
Collected data is used solely to:
- Upload and manage your videos on TikTok
- Provide user authentication

4. **Data Sharing**
No sharing, selling, or renting of personal data to third parties.

5. **Data Retention & Security**
Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary. We employ appropriate measures to protect your data.

6. **User Rights**
You can revoke access by:
- Deleting your account on the App
- Revoking permission from TikTok’s connected apps page

7. **Children**
The App is not intended for users under 13 years old.

8. **Changes to Policy**
We may update this Policy; changes will be effective upon posting.
